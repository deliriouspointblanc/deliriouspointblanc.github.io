The Start of Smthng Beautiful (2022)

Exploring poetry and text in Blender

The first of a series of works where I play with my poems as a sort of narrative for a 3D world.

These GAN generated videos are based off of a small dataset of trees, forest, skies. These were experiments for a comission that didn't come through, so I decided to use them myself.
