About

delirious.blanc is the sonic alias of Yasmin Morgan — a London-based DJ, creative technologist, and genre-bender. Their fast-paced sets fuse ecstatic vocals with hard-hitting rhythms from around the world. Genres flow between (but not limited to) future bass, garage, footwork, hyperpop, and vocal edits with intense energy and unexpected moments.
