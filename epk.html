About

Delirious.Blanc is the sonic alias of Yasmin Morgan — a London-based DJ, creative technologist, and genre-blender. Their fast-paced sets fuse ecstatic vocals with hard-hitting rhythms from around the world. Genres flow between (but not limited to) future bass, garage, footwork, hyperpop, and vocal edits with [emotional intensity and cinematic edge].

With roots in club experimentation and XR design, they brings an immersive, tactile approach to every mix — whether live or broadcast.
